                  Launching Exclusively On Hiteria Village

                  Octave is a Music Mode. Very similar to Fortnite Festival. It will launch first on Hiteria Village with 6 Tracks. I plan on eventually making it singleplayer and uploading it to Modrinth. But for now it will be exclusive to Hiteria. We plan on releasing at least 4 music tracks a week, These tracks will be exclusive to Hiteria. We don't plan on uploading any songs outside of Hiteria Village at the moment. We actively look through the music suggestions chat on Discord so if there is any songs you want added post it in Discord.
